About Me

Creative Philosophy

My creative focus is on personal and emotional storytelling, especially moments where people are dealing with pressure, change, memory, or something they cannot easily say out loud. I am interested in the connection between music, image, and mood,because sound often helps me understand the emotional rhythm of a scene before I fully understand it visually. I like filming real people, real places,and small details, because I feel that everyday moments can become cinematic when they are observed carefully.
